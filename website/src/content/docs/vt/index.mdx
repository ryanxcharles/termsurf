Programs running in terminals use **control sequences** to interact with the
terminal. Control sequences are a series of special characters that the terminal
interprets as commands. These are sometimes referred to as **VT sequences** or
**escape codes**.

This section documents the control sequences TermSurf's terminal supports.

> [Ghostty](https://ghostty.org)'s VT documentation, used under the MIT license.
> TermSurf's terminal (Ghostboard) is a Ghostty fork and inherits its VT engine.
